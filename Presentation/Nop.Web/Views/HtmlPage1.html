Real Oak Wood Furniture

At Oak Furniture Land we take pride in the fact that all our furniture is made from real wood, we do not use chipboard or mdf on any of our furniture. You will notice the characteristics of the wood and craftsmanship are showcased throughout the designs, something that you may not see from other retailers.

We offer a wide range of designs in real wood and all our collections have been created for multiple rooms of the home. For example our Original Rustic oak range encompasses furniture for the living, dining, home office and dining room. This collection is slightly distressed in a medium oak and would make a striking addition to any home.

If a more contemporary design is to your liking then the clean lines of the Tokyo natural oak range, featuring bowed tops and clean cut groves, is for you. This range has always been a popular choice with our customers and is a stunning example of modern oak furniture. Staying with this theme, but perhaps more aptly described as ultra modern furniture, are some of the pieces from Mantis Light natural mango furniture collection. The striking mango bookcases are simplistic in design while still showing off the natural beauty of the wood.

Furniture fashion never stands still and we are constantly expanding our ranges so be sure to check out our latest products.

So if you have just moved in or looking for ideas on how to update your home then be sure to make Oak Furniture Land a destination along the way!
